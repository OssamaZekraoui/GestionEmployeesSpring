Employees Management

Welcome to our homepage!

As an administrator, you can manage employees and jobs.

Employee Management:
  • To add an employee, go to the "Add Employee" page.
  • To modify employee information, go to the "Edit Employee" page.
  • To delete an employee, go to the "Delete Employee" page.
Job Management:
  • To add a job, go to the "Add Job" page.
  • To modify job details, go to the "Edit Job" page.
  • To delete a job, go to the "Delete Job" page.

Feel free to contact support if you have any further questions.
